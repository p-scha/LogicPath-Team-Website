What is LogicPath?

LogicPath is a gamified educational resource on logic.

By turning logic into a skill you can level up, LogicPath can help any willing learners sharpen their reasoning abilities.

It is a skill that permeates academics, personal decision-making, civic engagement, and professional problem-solving, improving outcomes across multiple areas of life.

The Problem:

“Despite logical thinking being an incredibly important skill, there are no comprehensive, yet engaging and accessible resources on logic.”

The currently existing online educational resources on logic may roughly fall in one of these two categories:

1. Is engaging, yet fails to cover logic with sufficient depth and structure

2. Sufficiently covers logic, yet is dry and unengaging
